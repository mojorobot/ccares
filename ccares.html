We are Glad You are Here

We gather on Sundays at 9:30am and 11:00am.

We would love for you to join us as we gather to worship our savior, Jesus Christ. From loving our Kids to growing in Life Groups to supporting Missions and Outreach, everything we do is centered around the Gospel of Jesus. That is the good news that though we were dead in our sins, Jesus paid our penalty by his death on the cross, giving us rightneousness in the eyes of God.
